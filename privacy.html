Privacy Policy

Insurance Jargon Decoder Chrome Extension | Last updated: March 29, 2026

Summary

The Insurance Jargon Decoder extension collects no user data. It makes no network requests. All functionality is entirely local to your browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically:

  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • No browsing history is recorded or transmitted
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry of any kind
  • No cookies are set
  • No network requests are made (all data is bundled within the extension)
  • No third-party scripts or services are used

Local Storage

The extension stores one setting locally on your device using Chrome's storage API: whether the auto-highlight feature is enabled or disabled. This setting never leaves your browser and contains no personal information.

Page Content Access

The extension reads text content on web pages solely to identify and highlight insurance terms. This processing happens entirely within your browser. No page content is collected, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Third Parties

This extension does not share data with any third parties because it does not collect any data.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The extension's core commitment to zero data collection will not change.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the extension developer through the GitHub issues page.
