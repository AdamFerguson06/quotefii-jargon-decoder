How It Works

Shopping for auto insurance and confused by the terminology? This free extension decodes insurance jargon right on the page, so you can make informed decisions without opening a new tab.

Auto-highlight

On insurance websites, recognized terms are automatically underlined. Hover or click to see the definition instantly.

Right-click anywhere

Select any insurance term on any website, right-click, and choose "Define Insurance Term" for an instant explanation.

Full glossary

Click the toolbar icon to browse and search all 50+ terms. A quick reference you can access anytime.

Terms Included

Covers the most common auto insurance terms, from basic coverage types to policy details. Each definition is written in plain English with real examples.
